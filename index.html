WHAT ARE SOME WELL KNOWN TERMS IN THE WORLD OF TECHNOLOGY?

This is a thing most people should know. Allow me to discuss well-known terms within the world of technology.
Software- These programs are terms given to specific applications which allow us to perform a specific task

Software Engineers - Its the term given to the people who are well-known in the language of the computer or programming languages and perform tasks which allow people to interact with computers. Or in simple words they are super smart people who act as translators for a computer to talk to a human being.
Hackers- These are people who break into software through small cracks which were made by software engineers while making a program. They use these entry point to further their personal interests. Interestingly, people hire hackers to secure their systems, in such cases they are called security specialists 🤯🤯.
